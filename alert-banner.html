Seattle University is Open and Operating on a Regular Schedule

Seattle University is open and operating on a regular schedule for all classes, business operations, and activities today, Thursday, December 1st. Please use caution when navigating the campus, given cold and wet conditions.

In all cases, faculty, staff, and students who live off-campus should use their best judgment about how safe it is to travel based on the conditions near their homes. Please inform your supervisor or professor (by e-mail or phone) if weather conditions impact your ability to be in the office or class.
